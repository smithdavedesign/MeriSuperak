Artist.
Therapeutic Music Practitioner.
Composer.

Play Therapeutic Music at the Bedside for PT's at Mercy General Hospital in Sacramento. In addition, I play Therapeutic Music with Music Partners In Healthcare (MPIH). Please look up the following websites: www.mpih.com and www.harmonybyhearth.com.
