About Me

As a Certified Therapeutic Music Practitioner I provide unobtrusive live music sessions – patients receive soothing music as a service, not as entertainment. As a Singer/songwriter, Musical Director and Multi-instrumentalist I tease new impressions from Modern, Classic, Baroque, Romantic and Celtic music, resulting in a smooth and jazzy sound.

My music sets a mood that invites the listener to feel good through laughter, reflection and opening up to the relaxing sounds created.
